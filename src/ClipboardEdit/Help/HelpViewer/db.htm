To get help in a dialog box

 Click the question mark    in the upper-right corner of the dialog box, and then click an item in the dialog box.

Notes

  • To close the pop-up window, click anywhere on the screen.
  • If the dialog box does not have the  , click Help, or press F1.
  • You can also get help on an item by right-clicking it.
  • Not all dialog boxes include dialog box help.
